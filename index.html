Lumio

Lumio is a new puzzle game for iOS developed using Cocos2D.

Screenshots

Description

Act fast before the lights go out!

Navigate across the lit platforms to collect stars and level up. But be quick, because when the lights go out you'll have to find a different route. Fight against the clock in this fast-paced puzzle game, take too long and it's game over!

Can't find a route? Collect power ups to restore platforms that have disappeared.

Compare high scores with your friends on GameCenter!
